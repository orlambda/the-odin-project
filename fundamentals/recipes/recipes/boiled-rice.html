Boiled Rice

Description

I love rice. My rice cooker was damaged when my kitchen flooded because it was on the floor for some reason, so I have been cooking rice in a pan. I use a small pan that fits this rice perfectly. The rice may need a quick shake or stir in the middle of cooking but otherwise try not to disturb it.

Ingredients

  • White rice, 1 cup
  • Water, 1.1 cups
  • Salt, just a tiny bit

Steps

  1. Rinse and drain the rice a few times until the water runs clear.
  2. Place the rice in a saucepan, along with the water and salt.
  3. Cook on a medium/high heat until the water gets close to boiling.
  4. Reduce to a low heat and cook for approx 10 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat, leaving the pan with the lid on for a few minutes before serving.
